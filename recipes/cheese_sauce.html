Vegan Cheese Sauce

Description: Homemade Cheese Sauce for Anything

This recipe will simplify your life. You can use it for pizza, burgers, nachos, macaroni, and much more.

It is a simple as boiling, blending, and seasoning. Let's get ready for some fun!!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Tumeric
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Nutritional Yeast
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Bring slightly salted water to a boil
  2. Add chopped potatoes, onions, and garlic to boil until potatoes are easily pierced by a fork
  3. Strain the vegetables and leave about 2/3 a cup of broth to help blend
  4. Add ingredients to a blender
  5. Add seasonings
  6. Add lemon juice and nutritional yeast to your liking
