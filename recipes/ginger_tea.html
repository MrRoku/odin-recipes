Ginger Tea

Description

Warm and soothing ginger tea. Great for at night, for an upset stomach or sore throat.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Ginger
  • Water
  • Sugar or Agave

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Crush and chop ginger
  3. Steep in boiling water
  4. Sweeten to your liking
  5. Let it cool before consuming
