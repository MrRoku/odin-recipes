Vegan Cheese Sauce

Description: Quick Nachos

Tasty and Spicy Vegan Nachos

Ingredients

  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cheese Sauce
  • Vegan Crumble
  • Onion
  • Peppers
  • Jalapeños
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375F
  2. Heat up crumble with chopped onions and peppers
  3. Spread tortilla chips on a trap
  4. Cover with cheese sauce and crumble with veggies
  5. Sprinkle jalapeños
  6. Heat in oven for 25-40 mins
  7. Add seasoning to taste
  8. Serve with guacamole and Salsa
